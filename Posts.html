Top 5 Must Places to Run in the USA

#1 Oahu, Hawaii

Run the beautiful trails of Oahu from sun up to sundown. Feel magic of the sand, jungle and ancient tree roots under your feet!

#2 Grand Canyon, Arizona

What could be more fun than running from one end of the worlds largest canyon to the other? Be prepared there are many areas where water isn't available.

#3 Silver Falls, Oregon

Oregon is home to hundreds of trails, but Silver Falls state park stands above the rest. Get the best of rivers, forest and mountains all in one!

#4 San Juan Mountain Range, Colorado

The SanJuan Mountain range is home to some of the best trails in the world. Colorado keeps this gem well hidden for the bold at heart. Good luck traversing this beauty!

#5 Ice Age Scenic Trail, Wisconson

The ice trails of Wisconson are to die for. The lakes, rivers and cliffs have an ancient feel to them. Embrace the wild as you trek through miles of well documented trails!
